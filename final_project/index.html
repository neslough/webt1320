MoJhoSto

A Casual Magic: the Gathering Format

MoJhoSto, a variant of the popular Momir Basic format, is a fun, chaotic way to play Magic: the Gathering. Typically to play Magic a player constructs a deck of various cards with different effects in order to achieve a particular purpose. Barring that, some 'limited' formats have players build their decks from a certain selection of cards just prior to play. MoJhoSto takes a different approach. Rather than have players build actual decks, player use a collection basic lands, which do very little on their own, and a few special cards called Vanguards to select random cards to play. MoJhoSto is named for the three Vanguards each player is given: Momir Vig, Simic Visionary, Jhoira of the Ghitu, and Stonehewer Giant.

This video provides a good introduction the Momir Basic format, of which MoJhoSto is a variant.

This site serves three main purposes: to introduce the format of MoJhoSto, to explain what the Vanguards are, and to provide a simple interface to generate random cards as necessary for the format.

Vanguards

These are the vanguards in use in MoJhoSto.

Momir

Perhaps the most used Vanguard in this format, Momir gives players access to creatures, the most important type of card in the game.

Jhoira

Jhoira gives players access to instants and sorceries, allowing players to shake up the game with interesting spells.

Stonehewer Giant

Stonehewer Giant gives creatures random equipments, varying gameplay greatly.
