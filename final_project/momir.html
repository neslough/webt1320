Momir Vig, Simic Visionary

Momir is easily the heart of this format, as it allows for the creation of creatures. As the most common method of defeating opponents is to attack them with creatures, Momir goes a long way towards winning the game.

Every card in the game has what's called a mana value (formerly 'converted mana cost,' but that term has been superceded). For example, consider the following cards.

Farfinder costs 3 mana of any type.

Cultivate costs 2 mana of any type, and a single Green mana. So, in total it costs 3 mana.

Narset, Parter of Veils costs 1 mana of any type and two Blue mana. So, in total she costs 3 mana.

Therefore, all of the cards have mana value 3.

To use Momir, you pay an amount of mana of your choice, and he fetches you a random creature from among creatures with than mana value. However, there isn't an easy way to choose a random creature from among those that have been printed in the game.
