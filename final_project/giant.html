Stonehewer Giant

While the name of the format is MoJhoSto, it really makes more sense to talk about Stonehewer Giant directly after Momir. Equipments are special artifacts in the game that may be attached (or 'equipped') to creatures, usually giving those creatures a stats boost and/or new abilities. Blackblade Reforged is an example on an equipment.

Basically, whenever you get a creature with Momir (or, very rarely, through some other means), Stonehewer Giant givees that creature a random equipment with a lesser mana value.
