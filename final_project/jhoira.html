Jhoira of the Ghitu

Jhoira of the Ghitu, combined with the other two vanguards, applies a pretty chaotic shake-up to the typical play of just getting creatures and maybe equipments. In particular, Jhoira is capable of fetching cards such as Hour of Revelation which can clear all cards on the board, useful if your opponent has had better luck in their creatures from Momir.

Jhoira can fetch a variety of other spells as well, though.

Vraska's Contempt could be useful to remove a particularly problematic creature, and since Momir and Jhoira both require mana, and that their controller discards a card, a spell like Eureka Moment can be useful both to refill your hand and to add extra mana for the next activation of Momir or Jhoira
